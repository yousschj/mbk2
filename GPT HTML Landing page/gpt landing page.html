Immersive English Language Learning

At Maple Bear Kenitra, we don't just teach English - we teach in English. Our immersive language program helps students become proficient in English and prepares them for success in a globalized world.

Play-Based Learning

We believe that children learn best through play. Our curriculum is designed to create a playful and engaging learning environment where students can develop their creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Safe and Secure Environment

At Maple Bear Kenitra, the safety and well-being of our students is our top priority. We provide a secure environment where children can explore, learn, and grow with hands-on activities.

Holistic Education

We are committed to developing our students' academic and personal potential. Our curriculum encourages curiosity, engagement, and fosters the development of well-rounded individuals.

Quality Assurance

Our teachers are trained by Canadian experts and our programs are constantly evaluated to ensure the highest standards of quality education.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our facilities are equipped with the latest technology and resources to support the best possible education for our students.

Welcome to Maple Bear School

For Parents

  • Ideal parents are those who value quality education and want to provide their children with the best possible start in life.
  • Maple Bear School is designed for parents who want their children to develop strong language skills, critical thinking abilities, and a global perspective.
  • The program is based on the Canadian education system, known for its high academic standards and innovative teaching methods.
  • The curriculum emphasizes English language arts, math, science, and technology, as well as personal and social development, creative activity, and physical skills and well-being.
  • Maple Bear School provides a safe and nurturing environment where children can grow and thrive.

For Students

  • Ideal students are curious, enthusiastic, and eager to learn.
  • Maple Bear School is perfect for students who want to improve their English language skills while also developing a wide range of academic and social abilities.
  • Students will enjoy hands-on learning experiences in a fun and engaging classroom setting.
  • Maple Bear School will help students build strong foundations in language arts, math, science, and technology, while also encouraging creativity, physical activity, and personal growth.
  • Students will make new friends and gain valuable skills that will prepare them for success in school and beyond.
